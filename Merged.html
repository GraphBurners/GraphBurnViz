Introduction to Graph Burning

Graph burning is a theoretical model for spreading information or influence through a network. The process follows specific rules:

  • Initial State: All vertices start as unburned
  • Burning Process: In each round:
    • A new unburned vertex is selected as a fire source
    • Fire spreads to all adjacent unburned neighbors of currently burning vertices
  • Burning Number: The minimum number of rounds needed to burn the entire graph
